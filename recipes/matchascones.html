Matcha Scones

Description

Sweet and simple matcha scones recipe with icing.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp matcha powder
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients.
  2. Incorporate cold butter into flour mixture.
  3. Add wet ingredients.
  4. Knead and form into 1-1.5 inch thich disk.
  5. Cut into slices and brush with an egg wash.
  6. Sprinkle with sugar.
  7. Bake at 350 for 14-17 min (slightly browned).
